Free · Local · GPU-accelerated

Edit your Twitch VODs at desktop speed.

WeEdit is a free video editor built for the thing browser editors choke on: trimming, cutting and exporting multi-gigabyte VODs. Native ffmpeg and your GPU do the heavy lifting — no uploads, no waiting, all local.

Want to look before you install? The web editor runs the real WeEdit interface right in your browser. Export, VOD download, NAS import and saving to disk are desktop-only — that's where the native performance lives.

Built for VOD-sized footage

Multi-track timeline

Video, audio and text tracks with cut, split, trim, snapping and per-clip volume & opacity.

Pull VODs directly

Sign in with Twitch and download a VOD straight into your library via yt-dlp — no manual exporting.

Native ffmpeg export

Export to MP4 with quality presets and NVENC GPU encoding. Scrub originals; no slow browser transcoding.

Text & overlays

Titles, captions and lower-thirds with live preview, plus stock video, images and royalty-free audio.

Import from anywhere

Local files, drag-and-drop, or connect to a NAS by share or IP to pull footage off your network.

Auto-save & updates

Folder-based projects that save as you work, and a built-in updater that keeps you on the latest build.

Web preview vs. the desktop app

Same editor, two ways to run it. The browser copy is for trying the interface instantly; the desktop app is the real tool.
